About Me

My name is Brillian Bagus Fategar, born in Kebumen, on March 13, 2001. I am the first of two siblings. My father is a businessman, while my mother is a homemaker.

When I was 6 years old, I started my education at SDN Wanasari 01. After graduating from there, I continued my education at Mts Nur El-Ghazy in 2013. After graduating from Mts in 2016, I then pursued my education at SMK Tridaya Bekasi in the field of Light Vehicle Engineering. After graduating from SMK, I started working at a chemical company in Cikarang as a Logistics Staff. In addition to my work, I am currently continuing my studies at Pelita Bangsa University (UPB), majoring in Information Technology.
